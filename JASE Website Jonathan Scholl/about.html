About us


What is Jase?

Jase is a multiplayer game to play with your friends outside. Divide your friends in 2 teams and compete with each other over fun challenges all around your area. Take pictures or videos for the other team, to prove your actions during the game. Get points for each confirmed challenge. Whoever gets the most points after a previously defined time wins the game!
